ABOUT ME!

Hello everyone! Welcome To NehaSharma205!!

I made this website because I have just started coding and I was advised to make a website on ads. So, I thought, why not create a website showcasing my own books? Yes, the books in the website are written by me! People get surprised when they come to know this but it is easy really. You just have to get started and you wouldn't even know when you are done with the book. So, if you write any books, hit me up on wattpad and probably I will read the book and give you a review while you can read mine.

So, I use the pen name of Neha Sharma to write my books. That is not my real name but you can still call me Neha and I would respond. I am from Mumbai and I believe that people outside as well as inside India are very ignorant about the Indian culture. That is why, I have decided to write books which show my culture, good and the bad and the possible reasons in my own way. I strive to educate everyone on the Indian culture through the bookish media the way people understand the American culture through the movie media (Hollywood).

In this website, I will provide information about my ongoing and completed novels, which I hope you would like. I am dedicating my time and efforts in writing these books so I really hope you would enjoy them. There is a review section below every book section and I would look forward to your reviews on my writing content.

The books are currently free so that broke students like me can read the books too because I know how much of a bummer it is when you can't keep reading something you like BUT I want to remove my broke status too so if I do manage to grab myself some good royalty program and get some much needed cash, I will surely update that on this webpage so that you won't read it halfway and be left hanging for more.

Besides all this professional stuff, it is nice meeting you all and thank you so much for visiting this webpage.

Please do not advertise about your books in the review sections of this webpage. That's not why I made this website. So, please refrain from talking about your own books here. YOU HAVE BEEN WARNED!! If you do, I won't hesitate to delete your comments.
